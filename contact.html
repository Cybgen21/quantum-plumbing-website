Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

Our emergency response team aims to reach you within 30 minutes for genuine emergencies. We have engineers on standby 24/7 across Staffordshire with fully equipped vehicles.

Do you charge for quotes and estimates?

All quotes and estimates are completely free with no obligation. For complex projects, we offer free site visits to provide the most accurate pricing.

Are all your engineers Gas Safe registered?

Yes, every engineer working on gas appliances is Gas Safe registered and carries their certification. We can provide registration numbers upon request.

What warranty do you provide on your work?

We provide a comprehensive 12-month warranty on all workmanship and installations. Parts warranties vary by manufacturer but are typically 2-10 years.
